My Story

Built From the Ground Up

I've spent my entire career inside the game — not just watching it, but studying it at every level. As a student manager at Miami (OH), I built a scouting and analytics department from scratch and helped run one of college baseball's most respected programs from the inside.

That led to Perfect Game USA, where I spent years evaluating amateur talent at showcases across the country — eventually becoming the National Scouting Coordinator, running evaluations from 13u all the way to draft-eligible prospects in Florida, Houston, Seattle, and Kansas City.

Then came the Chicago White Sox, where I spent two seasons as an Associate Scout covering the Midwest and Canada, learning how MLB organizations really evaluate the game — not just tools, but makeup, projectability, and the whole player.

Today I'm the Director of Recruiting and Assistant Pitching Coach at Eastern Michigan University, where I coach at the Division I level every day. I know what scouts want, what college coaches want, and what it actually takes to develop a pitcher or help a player find their path.

Private lessons are how I give that knowledge directly to you — no fluff, no gimmicks. Real baseball, real development.
