My Story

Built From the Ground Up

I've spent my entire career inside the game — not just watching it, but studying it at every level. As a student manager at Miami (OH), I built a scouting and analytics department from scratch and helped run one of college baseball's most respected programs from the inside out.

That led to Perfect Game USA, where I spent years evaluating amateur talent at showcases across the country — eventually becoming the National Scouting Coordinator, running evaluations from 13u all the way to draft-eligible prospects in Florida, Houston, Seattle, and Kansas City.

Then came the Chicago White Sox, where I spent two seasons as an Associate Scout covering the Midwest and Canada, learning how MLB organizations really evaluate the game — not just tools, but makeup, projectability, and the whole player.

Today I'm the Director of Recruiting and Assistant Pitching Coach at Eastern Michigan University, working with players at the Division I level every single day. I know what scouts want, what college coaches want, and what it actually takes to develop a player who earns their spot on the next level.

Virtual lessons are how I bring that knowledge directly to you — wherever you are. No fluff, no gimmicks. Real baseball, real development.

How It Works

The Virtual Lesson Process

Step 01

📹

Submit Your Film

Send me video of you pitching, hitting, catching, or playing defense — bullpen sessions, game footage, practice reps, whatever you've got. No fancy setup required.

Step 02

🔍

Detailed Breakdown

I review every rep through a coach's and scout's lens — identifying your strengths, the areas with the most room to grow, and what's holding you back from your next level.

Step 03

📋

Your Roadmap

You receive a concrete, personalized improvement plan — specific adjustments, drills, and focus areas mapped out in the order they'll make the most impact.

Step 04

🧠

Train the Mental Game

Every session includes mental skills work — the same mindset tools, routines, and frameworks we use at the college level, applied to your position and your game.

What I Offer

Lesson Areas

⚾

Pitching Development

My primary expertise. Send me your bullpen footage and I'll break down mechanics, arm path, pitch shape, command, sequencing, and workload management. Sessions are built around your specific arsenal and where you are in your development.

🧠 Mental game: mound presence, pitch-to-pitch focus, compete mentality, and pre-pitch routine.

Catching Instruction

Framing, blocking footwork, receiving mechanics, throwing, and game-calling — all broken down from your video. The craft of catching is undercoached at every level. I played catcher coming up and I know what good looks like behind the plate.

🧠 Mental game: managing a pitching staff, staying locked in each inning, reading the opponent.

8u–18u Framing Blocking Game-Calling

Hitting & Fielding

Swing mechanics, plate approach, and defensive fundamentals — all evaluated through your film. I've watched and graded thousands of at-bats from 8u to pro, and I know what separates players who advance from those who plateau.

🧠 Mental game: at-bat approach, two-strike adjustments, defensive focus and preparation.

Recruiting Guidance

As a D1 recruiting coordinator and former Perfect Game national coordinator, I've been on both sides of this process. I help players and families understand the recruiting landscape — how to build a profile, approach schools, evaluate fit, and navigate the timeline correctly.
